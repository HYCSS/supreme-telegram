  2. ×2 Divide into two teams.
  3. There are three rounds. Each round you must select a different tribute to act out the word on the card.
  4. You have 90 seconds to act out as many cards as possible. Use the space bar to move to the next card.
  5. You can only move to the next card when your team has guessed correctly.
